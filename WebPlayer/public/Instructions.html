Hand
You will have 4 cards in your hand at all times with the 4 types (Protein, Vegetables, Utensils and Sides)
DropZone
You will need to drag and drop the cards to the dropzones which are the white containers that appear at the center of the screen. You may change the cards between dropzones but you cannot return any card to your hand
Deck
Any time you drop a card in any dropzones you need to click the deck button so that the remaining number of cards that needs your hand, are added.
Score
Any time you get a combination right. The nutritional value is going to be added and counted as your current score. You can find it at the right top corner of the screen.
Time
You will have 5 minutes to make as many right combinations as possible. You can find it at the right top corner of the screen just below the score.
Combinations
We provide you a cheetsheet of the possible combinations you can make. You can find the button in the middle left side of the screen. This button is available at all times during the game.
Menu
You can click the menu button at any time to go back to the main menu, this button is found at the top left corner of the screen.
Special Cards
To add some fun, you can find special cards into your hand, such as add time or loose points.
